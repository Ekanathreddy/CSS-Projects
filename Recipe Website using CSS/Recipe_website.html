Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe:

  1. Take 4 slices of bread and remove the crusts. Blend the bread into crumbs. Add 1 tablespoon of milk powder
    and mix well. Add 3 tablespoons of milk and knead a soft dough. Form the dough into small, smooth balls.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
  3. In a saucepan, heat 2 cups of water and add 1 cup of sugar.
  4. Add the fried balls to the hot syrup and simmer for 2 minutes.
